The variables in the dataset are as follows:

Quantity of cement (kg/m^3)
Quantity of fly ash (kg/m^3)
Quantity of water (kg/m^3)
Type of recycled aggregate (Categorial variable)
Quantity of natural coarse aggregate (kg/m^3)
Quantity of natural fine aggregate (kg/m^3)
Quantity of recycled coarse aggregate (kg/m^3)
Quantity of recycled fine aggregate (kg/m^3)
Concrete age (day)
Compressive strength (MPa)
